# Welcome to Cboard

Cboard is a free web application for children and adults with speech and language impairments, facilitating communication with pictures and text-to-speech.

## What is Cboard?

Cboard is an augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) tool that helps people with communication difficulties express themselves. It uses a grid-based interface where users can select symbols, pictures, or words to build messages that are then spoken aloud by the device.

## Who Can Use Cboard?

Cboard is designed for:
- **Children and adults** with speech and language impairments
- **Students** in special education programs
- **Therapists and educators** working with communication support
- **Families** supporting loved ones with communication needs
- **Anyone** who needs an alternative way to communicate
